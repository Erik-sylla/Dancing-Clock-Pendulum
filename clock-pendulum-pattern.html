Physics
The weight hangs on a string of fixed total length tied at both hand-tips A and B. It falls to the lowest reachable point, found by ternary search over the along-chord parameter s, with droop depth h solved at each s. When the string is shorter than dist(A,B) the weight snaps to the lower tip.
